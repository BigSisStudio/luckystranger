Lucky — Moodboard

Lucky

Optimistic. Warm. Hand-painted. This direction leans into the unexpected good fortune that comes with being "lucky," which is a big part of your origin story. For those looking to see where the night takes them, this is the place to be. Utilitarian bones, dive bar DNA, a logo that looks like it's been above the door for twenty years and belongs there. It doesn't try to be cool. It just is.

Stranger — Moodboard

Stranger

Mid-century refined. Quietly knowing. This direction focuses on the "stranger" — the person at the end of the bar who feels familiar, but you can't quite place. Someone you can trust. The space is intriguing and intentional, with a visual expression that has nostalgic charm. This stranger is mysterious in the best possible way.
